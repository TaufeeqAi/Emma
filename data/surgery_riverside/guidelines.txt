RIVERSIDE HEALTH PRACTICE — Patient Information

OPENING HOURS
Monday, Wednesday, Friday: 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM
Tuesday, Thursday: 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM
Saturday: Closed
Sunday: Closed

APPOINTMENTS
Urgent same-day appointments: Available — call at 9:00 AM sharp
Standard appointments: 2–3 weeks wait currently
Online consultations: Available Tuesdays and Thursdays
Routine health checks: Book in advance via NHS App

EMERGENCIES
Dial 999 for life-threatening emergencies.
Out-of-hours urgent care: Call 111 for guidance.
Walk-in centre: Riverside Walk-In Centre, open 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily.

PRESCRIPTIONS
All repeat prescription requests require 72 hours notice.
Online requests via NHS App preferred.
Paper requests accepted at reception before 11:00 AM.
Controlled drugs: In-person request with valid photo ID required.

EXTENDED ACCESS
Evening appointments: Available Tuesday and Thursday until 7:00 PM.
Weekend access: Contact NHS 111 for weekend GP service.

TEST RESULTS
Results available 5–7 working days after testing.
Request results via the NHS App or by calling reception after this window.

MENTAL HEALTH CRISIS
Dial 999 or go to A&E if you or someone else is in immediate danger.
Samaritans: 116 123 (free, 24/7, confidential).
Non-urgent mental health concerns: Speak to your GP at your next appointment.

PATIENT REGISTRATION
New patient registration: Open to residents within the Riverside catchment area.
Bring two forms of ID and proof of address.

DISABLED ACCESS
Fully accessible premises. Hearing loop available at reception desk.
Parking: Two disabled parking spaces directly outside the main entrance.

COMPLAINTS
To raise a concern: speak with our Practice Manager or submit a written request.
